About

I am passionate about Technology and Data Science, with a focus on AI and machine learning applied in the pharmaceutical industry. I have 4 years of industry and academic experience in applying computational techniques and statistical analyses to scientific data.

Work experience

  • Intern in Statistics Jul 2023 - Oct 2023
    Johnson&Johnson, Basel, Switzerland
    I was based in the Statistics and Decision Sciences (SDS) department, within the Manufacturing and Applied Statistics (MAS) group. My main contribution was the development of a machine learning (ML) algorithm for the analysis of chromatography data, together with an R shiny application for the user interface.

  • Intern in Data Science Mar 2020 - Aug 2021
    Roche, Basel, Switzerland
    I carried out my thesis work in the In-vivo Electrophysiology group in the Neuroscience Research department. My work focused on the analysis of time series data from accelerometer and Electroencephalogram (EEG) signals with supervised and unsupervised Machine Learning algorithms.

Academic experience

  • PhD Computer Science Sep 2021 - Aug 2025
    University of Bern, Bern, Switzerland
  • Master's in Physics Oct 2018 - Apr 2021
    University of Padova, Padova, Italy
  • Bachelors's in Physics Oct 2015 - Sep 2018
    University of Padova, Padova, Italy

Research

©Neuron volume 107

In my PhD I use Data Science and Machine Learning techniques to make sense of complex brain data. Usually, this data takes the form of multivariate electrophysiological signals, for which computational models are needed.
